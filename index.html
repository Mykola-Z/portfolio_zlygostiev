About Me

I defended my PhD in December of 2019 at the University of Paris Saclay. My dissertation is titled "Development and optimization of mechanical polishing for superconducting accelerating cavities". During my PhD studies I investigated the influence of mechanical polishing techniques, in particular metallographic polishing and centrifugal barrel polishing, on Niobium, which is used as the main material to build the superconducting accelerating cavity.
